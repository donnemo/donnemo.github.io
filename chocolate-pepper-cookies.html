Geeky Muse: Chocolate Pepper Cookies

Andrew Paulson

Here’s a recipe for a spiced chocolate cookie with black pepper.

Yield: 2 and 1/2 dozen cookies
Cook Time: 12-15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 3/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground allspice
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 and 1/2 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a large bowl, cream butter, black pepper, cinnamon, allspice, and vanilla. Beat in sugar and continue beating until light and fluffy. Add in the lightly beaten egg. Sift flour and cocoa into a separate bowl; then slowly mix well with the butter and sugar mixture until a firm dough comes together.

Lightly flour hands and rool spoonfulls of dough into 1 inch balls. Place the dough about 1 inch apart on greased cookie sheet and bake on middle rack for 12-15 minutes or until firm. Cool for a minute on cookie sheet; then transfer to a cooling rack. Once cooled, dust with powdered sugar, if desired. Store in an airtight container.
